Sahu Pokhar Muz

Sahu Pokhar, located in Muzaffarpur Bihar, is a historic site featuring a temple and a pond, built in 1757 by Shivshahay Prasad Sahu.The temple, a Vishnu Panchayatean complex dedicated to deities like Radha Krishna, Lord Shiva and Suryadev.

ASHOKA PILLAR

The Ashoka Pillar is a Famous spot in Vaishali, located near the coronation Tank. It is a pillar with lion carvings, built by king Ashoka. Nearby, there is also a brick stupa, which is believed to mark the place where Lord Buddha gave his last sermon.
